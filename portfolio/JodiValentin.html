Jodi Valentin - What You Desire ( Inpinseptipin Remix )

Project information

  • Category: Compositions
  • Project Type: Tracks
  • Project date: 06 May, 2016

This track was my submission to a remix contest held by Jodi Valentin and hosted by Indaba Music. The contest involved remixing the track by the artist Jodi Valentin, called What you Desire. The track talks about how she is still in love with the person that she used to be in a relationship with, that she still desires to be with that person. How love is the cure and answer to everything. I wanted to retain its essence and build more on the backtrack. Retaining the same tempo and its instrumental stems and samples, I composed a new melody to sit over the dynamic bass instrumental and adopted a commercial EDM song structure for arrangement. The result was well received by the community and the song was voted second in the competition.
