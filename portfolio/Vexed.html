Inpinseptipin - Vexed (Original Mix)

Project information

  • Category: Compositions
  • Project Type: Originals
  • Project date: 20 May, 2019

I started the project in 2017. It started as a project to test out low frequency growls and how to incorporate them in my tracks. The first break and the drop was created on the day i started with the project. The subsequent parts took more than a year.

  • Everyday like clockwork at 1.00 A.M , i used to open the project file and try to write the missing parts to finish the songs. The various versions were all derivatives of what was popular or "trendy" in the electronic music phase.
  • One day , i would try to insert vocal chops because every song at that time was using it, or i will push in atmospheric nuances such as sirens , chants because KSHMR was doing it too. I even lamented on changing the entire track so that it would feel relevant in the Electronic Music phase.
